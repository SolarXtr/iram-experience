# Executive Summary

iRAM (Intelligent Research Administration & Management) is a digital platform designed to modernize research administration through standardized processes, organizational knowledge management, analytics, and artificial intelligence.

The platform serves not only as a Research Management Information System (RMIS) but also as a long-term organizational reference model.

---

# Strategic Objectives

* Improve research administration efficiency.
* Preserve institutional knowledge.
* Support executive decision-making.
* Enable digital transformation.
* Establish a reusable organizational framework.
