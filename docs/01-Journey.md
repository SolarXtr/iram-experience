# Welcome

Welcome to the Universe of iRAM.

If **00-Prologue** explains *why* iRAM exists,

this document tells the story of *how* it came to life.

This is not merely a timeline of events.

It is the journey of ideas, decisions, people, and purpose that continue to shape the future of iRAM.

---

# Where Everything Began

iRAM did not begin with software.

It did not begin with code.

It began with a simple question.

> **How can research administration become more intelligent, more connected, and more sustainable?**

That question inspired a vision far greater than building another information system.

It became the beginning of a platform.

---

# The First Dashboard

The earliest ideas were simple.

A dashboard to summarize research activities.

A better way to present information.

A tool to support better decisions.

But every improvement revealed another opportunity.

One dashboard became many.

One report became an ecosystem.

# From RMIS to iRAM

Research Management Information System (RMIS) was the starting point.

The project gradually expanded beyond data management.

Documentation.

Analytics.

Artificial Intelligence.

Knowledge Management.

Automation.

Architecture.

Together, these ideas formed something much larger than RMIS.

They formed iRAM.
